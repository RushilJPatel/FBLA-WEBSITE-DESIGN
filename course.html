Our Projects & Services

Our Projects

Green Schools Initiative

Partnering with local schools to implement eco-friendly practices, promote environmental education, and create sustainable learning environments. This project aims to reduce waste, conserve resources, and instill a culture of sustainability among students and staff.

Community Garden Program

Establishing and supporting community gardens in urban areas to promote local food production, improve access to fresh produce, and educate communities about sustainable gardening practices. This project fosters community engagement, food security, and environmental stewardship.

Riverside Cleanup and Restoration

Organizing regular cleanup events along riversides or waterways to remove plastic waste, restore habitats, and raise awareness about the importance of preserving water ecosystems. This project involves community volunteers, local organizations, and educational outreach to address water pollution and protect aquatic environments.
